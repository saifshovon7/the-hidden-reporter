UFC 326 Results: Charles Oliveira Upsets Max Holloway with Dominant Grappling

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: CBS Sports

The main event of UFC 326 saw a stunning upset as Charles Oliveira defeated Max Holloway to claim the BMF title. Oliveira's grappling skills proved to be too much for Holloway, as he secured the victory in a dominant performance.

Advertisement

In the co-main event, Drew Dober closed out his bout against Michael Johnson with a devastating knockout in the second round. Dober opened the fight with a series of lead leg kicks, but Johnson landed a combination of punches to take the lead. Dober later switched to the inside leg kick and landed several shots before throwing a wide right hand that put Johnson to sleep. A single follow-up punch sealed the win, with the official result being Dober def. Johnson via second-round KO.

Oliveira's victory marked a significant upset in the BMF title fight, while Dober's win showcased his impressive knockout power.
