Apple Refreshes Leadership Page with New Executives

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: MacRumors

Apple has updated its leadership page with the addition of three new executive profiles: Jennifer Newstead, Molly Anderson, and Steve Lemay.

Advertisement

The company also made changes to the profile of Eddy Cue, updating his title to Senior Vice President of Services and Health and providing a new headshot.

Cue, a 34-year Apple veteran who joined the company in 1989, has taken on oversight of Apple's health and fitness teams since Jeff Williams' retirement last year.
