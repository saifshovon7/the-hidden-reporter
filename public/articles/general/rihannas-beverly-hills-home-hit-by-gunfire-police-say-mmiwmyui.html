Rihanna's Beverly Hills Home Hit by Gunfire, Police Say

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: BBC News

The Beverly Hills home of pop superstar Rihanna was hit by gunfire on Sunday, according to the Los Angeles Police Department.

Officers responded to reports of gunfire at 13:15 local time (21:15 GMT) and located a suspect, a woman in her 30s, who was taken into custody.

A police official confirmed that the home targeted belonged to Rihanna and that assault rifle casings were found at the scene.

No one was injured in the incident, despite Rihanna being present in the mansion at the time, according to a law enforcement source.

The suspect stopped in a car outside the home and fired seven shots before speeding away, police say.

Her vehicle was located about eight miles (12km) away from the singer's home, where she was taken into custody.

The incident comes after a series of high-profile events involving Rihanna and her partner, A$AP Rocky, including a recent pregnancy announcement and a trial in which A$AP Rocky was found not guilty of firing a gun at a former friend.

Rihanna, whose real name is Robyn Fenty, rose to prominence in the early 2000s with hits like Pon de Replay and Umbrella and has since launched multiple businesses, including Fenty Beauty and a lingerie company.

The 37-year-old's net worth has been estimated by Forbes at over a billion dollars.
