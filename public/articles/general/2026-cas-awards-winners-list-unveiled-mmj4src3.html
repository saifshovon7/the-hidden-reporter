2026 CAS Awards Winners List Unveiled

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: Deadline

At the 62nd annual CAS Awards, Warner Bros' F1 and Apple's F1 were the big film winners, taking the respective Live Action and Animated Motion Picture prizes.

Joseph Kosinski's racing drama starring Brad Pitt, which won Best Sound at the BAFTA Film Awards last week and is nominated for Best Sound at the Oscars next Sunday, also vied for the checkered flag.

Netflix's KPop Demon Hunters, the most-watched movie of all time, added to its awards haul by sweeping the Annie Awards last month and winning a CAS statuette for Non-Theatrical Motion Picture or Limited Series.

HBO Max drama The Pitt and Apple TV's The Studio took home awards for Television Series – One Hour and Half Hour, respectively.

A pair of musical nonfiction titles also took home hardware, with Becoming Led Zeppelin winning the Motion Picture Documentary category and Billy Joel: And So It Goes winning Television Non-Fiction, Variety or Music – Series or Specials.

Guillermo del Toro received the Cinema Audio Society Filmmaker Award and Oscar-winning Roma re-recording mixer Skip Lievsay accepted the CAS Career Achievement Award.

Here is the full list of winners:

  • MOTION PICTURES – LIVE ACTION: F1: The Movie
  • TELEVISION SERIES – ONE HOUR: The Pitt S01 E13 7:00 PM
  • MOTION PICTURES – DOCUMENTARY: Becoming Led Zeppelin
  • NON-THEATRICAL MOTION PICTURES OR LIMITED SERIES: Adolescence S01 E01
  • TELEVISION NON-FICTION, VARIETY or MUSIC – SERIES or SPECIALS: Billy Joel: And So It Goes S1 E01 Part One
