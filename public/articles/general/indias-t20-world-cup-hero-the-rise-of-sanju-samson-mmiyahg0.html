India's T20 World Cup Hero: The Rise of Sanju Samson

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: BBC News

The T20 World Cup final in Ahmedabad marked a pivotal moment in Sanju Samson's career, as India emerged victorious against New Zealand, with Samson being named the most valuable player of the tournament.

Samson's impressive performance in the final was a culmination of years of hard work and dedication, having started his journey in domestic Ranji trophy tournaments and later making a name for himself in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Advertisement

At 31, Samson has come a long way since his debut in 2015, initially as a product of the IPL with the Rajasthan Royals, and has shown remarkable patience and perseverance in the face of challenges.

Despite facing criticism and pressure, Samson has continued to trust his method and stay true to his conventional batting style, which has been built on precise footwork, balance, and timing.

His humility and dedication have been key factors in his success, as he has managed to balance his role as a wicketkeeper batter with the demands of the team.

Samson's journey serves as a testament to the power of hard work and determination, and his rise to becoming India's T20 World Cup hero is a story that will be remembered for years to come.
