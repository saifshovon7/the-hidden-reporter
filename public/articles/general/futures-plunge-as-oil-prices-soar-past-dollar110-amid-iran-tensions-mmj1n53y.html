Futures Plunge as Oil Prices Soar Past $110 Amid Iran Tensions

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 6, 2026 Source: Investor's Business Daily

Dow Jones futures declined significantly early Monday, with S&P 500 futures and Nasdaq futures also experiencing a sharp drop, as crude oil futures surged above $110 a barrel.

The Iranian government rejected calls for a cease-fire, while President Donald Trump maintained that the Iran conflict is popular with his base.

In a separate development, Iran's leadership announced the appointment of Mojtaba Khamenei, the son of the late Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, as the new leader.

The global markets are closely monitoring the situation in the Middle East, with oil prices continuing to rise in response to the escalating tensions.

As the situation remains volatile, investors and analysts are closely watching for any developments that could impact the global economy.
