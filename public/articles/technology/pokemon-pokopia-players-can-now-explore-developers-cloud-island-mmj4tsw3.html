Pokémon Pokopia Players Can Now Explore Developer's Cloud Island

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: Nintendo Life

The Pokémon Company has released a code allowing players to visit the developer's Cloud Island in Pokémon Pokopia.

To access the island, players must have an active Switch Online membership and the Mysterious Goggles, which can be purchased with Life Coins from the Pokémon PC shop.

Advertisement

The developer's Cloud Island contains possible spoiler content, so players should be aware of this before visiting.

In related news, a special event called 'More Spores for Hoppip' is currently taking place, and a Ditto rug is available via the Mystery Gift menu.

Players are encouraged to share their experiences with Pokémon Pokopia in the comments.
