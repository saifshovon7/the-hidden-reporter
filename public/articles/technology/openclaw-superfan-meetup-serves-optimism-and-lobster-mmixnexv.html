OpenClaw Superfan Meetup Serves Optimism and Lobster

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: The Verge

The OpenClaw superfan meetup served as a celebration of the AI assistant platform created by Peter Steinberger in November 2025.

Hosted at Ideal Glass Studios in Manhattan, the event drew hundreds of people who gathered to discuss and showcase the open-source tool.

OpenClaw has gained popularity in the tech industry for its open-source nature, setting it apart from AI agent services controlled by big labs like Google and OpenAI.

However, the platform still poses major security risks, making it an unpredictable tool.

Despite this, the community sees OpenClaw as a grassroots crusade and a noble pursuit, offering an escape from an industry controlled by a handful of people.

‘AI was controlled by the big labs,’ said Michael Galpert, one of the event’s hosts, ‘This is kind of a watershed moment where Peter kind of busted down the doors.’

The event was part of a global meetup tour, with previous events held in San Francisco, Miami, Austin, Tel Aviv, Tokyo, and Madrid.

The organizers spared no expense on a lavish buffet table, complete with lobster claws, lemons, and charcuterie boards.

Galpert, a member of the AI community with a background working on Fortnite for Epic Games, said the idea for the meetup came about via Discord.
