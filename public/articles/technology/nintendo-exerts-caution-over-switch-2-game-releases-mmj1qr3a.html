Nintendo Exerts Caution Over Switch 2 Game Releases

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: Nintendoeverything.com

New Blood Interactive's Dave Oshry shared his thoughts on Nintendo's approach to game releases on the Switch 2.

According to Oshry, Nintendo is being careful with which games it allows on the new console, citing a desire to avoid a 'giant slop fest' like the Switch 1 eShop.

Despite having development kits and a new version of Dusk ready to go, which runs at 120 FPS, New Blood Interactive has not received approval to launch the game.

Advertisement

Oshry noted that Nintendo is 'still pretty cagey about letting games launch on Switch 2' and is still choosing which studios get to release their games on the console.

The comments come after a recent report revealed that some studios are still struggling to obtain development kits for the Switch 2.

While Nintendo has been cooperative with New Blood Interactive, providing them with dev kits and allowing updates for their games, Oshry believes that the company's cautious approach is necessary to maintain the quality of games on the Switch 2.
