Apple's 2026 Product Pipeline: Upcoming Devices Revealed

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: 9to5Mac

Apple has wrapped up a busy week of announcements, but the company still has a dozen or so products in the pipeline for 2026.

According to Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, Apple is set to release an M5 Max and M5 Ultra Mac Studio in the near future, with the new Mac Studio expected to hit shelves in the first half of 2026.

In addition to the Mac Studio, Apple is working on a refreshed Mac mini and iMac, potentially with M6 chips, as well as a redesigned MacBook Pro with M6 Pro and M6 Max.

The company also announced the refreshed iPad Air with M4 chip this week, but a rumored budget iPad update with an A18 chip did not materialize.

However, Apple is still working on a new revamped iPad mini with OLED and A19 Pro, set to launch in the fall.

On the home front, Apple has three major products in the pipeline: a refreshed Apple TV, a refreshed HomePod mini, and an all-new smart home display, which many refer to as HomePad.

The updated Apple TV 4K is expected to incorporate an A17 Pro chip and support Apple Intelligence, while the refreshed HomePod mini will come with a newer chipset and potentially new colors, including red.

The biggest announcement of the bunch is likely to be the new smart home display, which will incorporate an A18 chip, a 7-inch display, and become the ultimate smart home hub for HomeKit and interacting with Siri.

Later this year, Apple is expected to unveil new flagship iPhones, Apple Watches, and potentially AirPods, including the iPhone 18 Pro and iPhone 18 Pro Max, which will debut with new color options, improved cameras, and slightly bigger batteries.

Apple will also introduce its first foldable iPhone, as well as the Apple Watch Series 12 and Ultra 4, which will feature new health sensors.
