iPhone 17E: Is the Extra Cost Worth It?

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: The Verge

The iPhone 17E is a notable upgrade over its predecessor, the 16E, with several key features, including MagSafe and a base storage bump up to 256GB. However, the question remains whether the additional cost is justified.

Advertisement

The 17E starts at $600, a price that may seem steep given the memory crisis and the existence of the iPhone 17, which offers a ProMotion display and an always-on display. The latter feature, in particular, is a significant upgrade that allows users to check notifications, widgets, and the time at a glance.

While the 17E is a better value than the 16E, the main thing to know is that the iPhone 17 exists, offering a more comprehensive set of features for an additional $200. If you have the means, the 17 may be worth the extra cost, but for others, the 17E may be a better option.
