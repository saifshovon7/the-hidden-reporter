Xbox Insider Denies Return of Exclusives Despite New CEO's Hints

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: TheGamer

Despite previous indications from Microsoft Gaming CEO Asha Sharma that exclusives could be returning to the Xbox brand, a reliable Xbox insider has asserted that won't ultimately be the case.

Taking to NeoGAF, SneakersSO, a trusted Xbox insider, stood firm that Xbox exclusive titles aren't returning in a thread discussing whether exclusivity could ultimately 'save' the next Xbox console.

The next Xbox console, dubbed Project Helix, was revealed by Microsoft on Thursday, and it will play PC games.

'Exclusives are absolutely not happening,' the insider wrote, a statement that contradicts Sharma's recent hints and makes their words particularly interesting.

Sharma had taken to social media to write, 'Hear you' in response to a gamer who noted that 'Xbox games must be only on Xbox,' but this statement does not confirm a strict exclusivity policy.

While it's possible to imagine a more stringent approach moving forward with Xbox titles, especially in light of Sony's decision to pull back from releasing single-player titles on PC, the dam has already been broken.

Already, both Gears of War and Forza Horizon 5 have made their way to PlayStation consoles, representing two of Xbox's core 'pillars.'

The third, Halo, has already been confirmed to debut on PlayStation via Halo: Campaign Evolved, a remake of the first Halo game.

Additionally, other titles like Doom, Avowed, and South of Midnight have already released on PlayStation or have future plans to do so, and Fable, a key tentpole exclusive, will also see a debut on PlayStation later this year.

Furthermore, the revenue generated from porting these titles to PlayStation hardware has been significant, with Alinea Analytics estimating that five million copies of Forza Horizon 5 sold on PlayStation alone, generating over $300 million in revenue.

The potential loss of this revenue might not be something Microsoft is wholly committed to, even if it meant goodwill from its fanbase.
