Hasbro CEO Chris Cocks Discusses AI, KPop Demon Hunters, and the Future of Harry Potter

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: The Verge

Hasbro's CEO, Chris Cocks, recently sat down for an interview, discussing the company's foray into artificial intelligence, the growing popularity of adult-oriented toys and games, and its recent partnership with the Harry Potter franchise.

Cocks mentioned that Hasbro is utilizing AI to help design toys, specifically citing an AI-powered Peppa Pig that has been assisting in the creative process. This move reflects the company's effort to stay ahead of the curve in terms of technology and innovation.

One of the most significant challenges facing Hasbro is the shift in the toy industry towards adult-oriented products. Cocks noted that this trend is driven by the fact that adults have more disposable income than children, and as a result, companies are catering to this demographic.

Hasbro is also investing heavily in digital media, including mobile games such as Monopoly Go, which has been a major success for the company. Additionally, the company is expanding its presence in the video game space through original titles like Exodus, which is set to release next year.

However, this expansion also comes with its own set of challenges, including the highly competitive video game market and the sensitive nature of intellectual property.

Cocks was also asked about Hasbro's recent partnership with the Harry Potter franchise, which will see the company distributing merchandise for the next several years. He acknowledged the importance of being mindful of fan sentiment and respecting the creators and stories that fans love.

Overall, Chris Cocks' interview provides insight into the current state of the toy industry and Hasbro's efforts to stay ahead of the curve in terms of technology and innovation.
