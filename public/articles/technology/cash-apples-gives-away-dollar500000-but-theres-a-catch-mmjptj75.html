Cash Apples Gives Away $500,000, But There's a Catch

Cash App has launched a web game called Cash Apples, where players can earn cash by clicking on virtual trees. However, the game has a daily earnings limit and requires registration on the waitlist.

The Hidden Reporter Staff · March 9, 2026 · Source: The Verge

Cash App, in partnership with MSCHF, has launched a web game called Cash Apples, where players can earn cash by clicking on virtual trees.

The game's objective is to collect golden apples, with a maximum daily earnings of $200 per day between 4PM ET and 8PM ET for a maximum of $1,000 over the week.

However, the game has an alternate entry form, as it is also a sweepstakes, and registration on the waitlist is required.

Advertisement

Players can earn a maximum of $200 per day, but no individual will strike it rich this way.

The game's rules state that players need to register their phone number on the waitlist to participate.

According to Cash App, the game is designed to drive signups, with $500,000 up for grabs over the next five days.

Similar to the 'click and win' games of the past, Cash Apples requires players to click on virtual trees to collect golden apples and earn cash.

However, players have reported experiencing issues with the game, including 'Unable to connect' messages.
