Behind the Scenes of OnlyFans: Life as a Low-Paid 'Chatter'

A Philippines-based woman has spoken out about the harsh realities of working as a low-paid 'chatter' on the online platform OnlyFans, describing the experience as 'heartbreaking' and 'icky'.

The Hidden Reporter Staff · March 11, 2026 · Source: BBC Technology

A Philippines-based woman has spoken out about the harsh realities of working as a 'chatter' on the popular online platform OnlyFans. The platform, which generated $7.2bn in revenue in 2024, connects creators of explicit content with users who pay for access to their material and online chats.

However, while high-profile creators can earn large sums of money, the job of interacting with fans and attempting to sell them images and videos is often done by low-paid individuals, employed by third-party agencies. These workers, known as 'chatters,' are typically paid less than $2 per hour and work long hours, often with little job security or benefits.

The woman, who wishes to remain anonymous, described her experience as 'heartbreaking' and 'icky.' She said that she was forced to engage in explicit conversations with fans, often pretending to be a more successful model. She also described the emotional toll of the job, including feelings of exploitation and moral compromise.

OnlyFans has declined to comment on the issue, citing its terms of service, which state that its business relationship is solely with the content creator. However, a union representing workers in the industry has expressed concerns about the 'largely unregulated nature' of online work.

The use of chatters has led to legal cases against OnlyFans and the agencies that employ them, with some users and law firms alleging that the practice is deceptive. However, none of these cases have been successful so far.

The woman's story highlights the need for greater transparency and regulation in the online adult entertainment industry. As the industry continues to grow and evolve, it is essential that workers are protected and treated fairly.
