Abu Dhabi's Judan Acquires Majority Stake in Alpha Wave Global

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: Bloomberg Technology

Alpha Wave Global, an alternative asset manager, holds stakes in prominent technology firms including SpaceX, Anthropic, and OpenAI.

Abu Dhabi's new financial-services holding company, Judan, has agreed to acquire a 50.1% majority stake in Alpha Wave Global.

Advertisement

The deal aims to accelerate the launch of Alpha Wave's AI-native life insurance business, according to the firms involved.

Financial details of the transaction have not been disclosed.

The acquisition marks the latest move by Gulf investors to expand their global presence, despite concerns over the potential impact of the Iran conflict on regional business activity.
