Apple Prepares High-End 'Ultra' Products

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: The Verge

Apple is reportedly preparing at least three new products that will fit into its highest-end 'ultra' lineup.

Fresh off launching the low-cost MacBook Neo, the tech giant is said to be working on a touchscreen MacBook Pro, which is expected to be released in the fall, and a foldable iPhone that could cost around $2,000.

Advertisement

More interesting are the next-gen AirPods, which are rumored to include cameras to feed visual context to Siri.

According to Bloomberg's Mark Gruman, the next batch of releases may not bear the 'ultra' name, but will all command price premiums over their mainline counterparts.

Between the Neo and multiple foldables in the works, it seems that Apple is simultaneously trying to go further up- and down-market.
