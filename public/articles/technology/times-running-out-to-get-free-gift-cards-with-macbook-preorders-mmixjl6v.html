Time's Running Out to Get Free Gift Cards with MacBook Preorders

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: The Verge

Time's running out to get a free gift card when you preorder a new MacBook. Apple announced a week of product launches, and some of the most anticipated tech is set to ship on March 11th.

With deals aplenty, including $50 off the new M4 iPad Air and 17 percent off Marathon on Steam, there's something for everyone. Whether you're in the market for new hardware or looking for a great gaming experience, this week has been a treat for tech enthusiasts.

Don't miss out on these amazing deals. Time's running out to get your free gift card, so act fast!
