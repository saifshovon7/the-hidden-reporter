Indie App Weather mini Offers Trip Forecasts and More

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: 9to5Mac

Welcome to Indie App Spotlight, a weekly series showcasing the latest apps in the indie app world. Weather mini is a beautifully designed weather app for the Apple ecosystem that adds key features missing from Apple Weather.

The app uses Apple's WeatherKit, ensuring reliable data, and includes features such as trip forecasts, on-device Apple Intelligence models for forecasts, and a fully featured Apple Watch app.

Advertisement

Users can plan trips and track the weather in advance, saving the information for easy access. Weather mini also offers a dock icon on Mac that tracks the weather in real-time, integration with Siri, and a beautiful Apple Watch app with complication support.

The app prioritizes user privacy and is available for free on the App Store for iPhone, iPad, Mac, Vision Pro, and Apple Watch. Weather mini Plus offers additional features, including live dock icon support, widgets, and multi-city support, for $1.49/month, $12.99/year, or $34.99 lifetime.
