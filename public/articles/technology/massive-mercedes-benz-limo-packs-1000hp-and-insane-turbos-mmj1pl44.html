Massive Mercedes-Benz Limo Packs 1,000hp and Insane Turbos

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: Supercarblondie.com

A former Mercedes-Benz E-Class limousine has been transformed into a 1,000hp beast, complete with massive twin-turbochargers and a Corvette V8 engine.

The car, owned by content creator Backfire Brad, was created in just four months to debut at the 2025 SEMA show.

The standard Mercedes E240 engine has been replaced with a twin-turbocharged 6.0-liter Corvette V8 engine, producing 1,022hp.

Advertisement

The car's exterior design features bright yellow paintwork and 'Drift Limo' logos, while its massive turbos are so large they protrude from the hood.

The limo has been put to the test at a drift event, where it showcased its impressive performance and sound.

With a huge rear wing and a penchant for drifting, this Mercedes-Benz limo is a far cry from its former life as a luxury vehicle.
