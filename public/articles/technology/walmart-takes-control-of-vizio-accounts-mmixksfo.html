Walmart Takes Control of Vizio Accounts

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: The Verge

Vizio accounts are being phased out as Walmart takes the next steps in formally integrating the TV maker into its operations.

After Walmart's acquisition of Vizio in 2024, customers are now being asked to sign in or create a Walmart account when purchasing a new Vizio TV.

According to an email sent by Vizio, customers have the option to merge their Vizio account with their Walmart account or delete it.

If a customer chooses to delete their account, they have 30 days to request a copy of any data related to the account, after which it 'may no longer be available.'

The option to merge accounts is currently only available on newly purchased TVs, but it will eventually be expanded to existing TV owners.

This move marks a significant step in Walmart's efforts to consolidate its operations and provide a seamless experience for customers across its various brands.
