Rugged Phone Features Detachable Action Camera

The RugOne Xsnap 7 Pro, a rugged phone from Ulefone, features a detachable camera that can be used as a standalone action camera. The phone was showcased at MWC 2026, but pricing and launch details remain unknown.

The Hidden Reporter Staff · March 9, 2026 · Source: The Verge

The RugOne Xsnap 7 Pro, a new rugged phone from Ulefone, boasts a unique modular magnetic accessory system with a detachable camera.

The detachable camera can be used as a standalone action camera, offering similar functionality to the Insta360 Go Ultra.

Advertisement

When detached, the phone serves as the camera's preview screen through a wireless connection, and when docked, it can download and store footage from the camera and recharge it using its own 9,000mAh battery.

The RugOne Xsnap 7 Pro was showcased at MWC 2026, but the company did not share any details about pricing or an official launch date.

The phone is expected to be powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 8400 5G chip with 12GB of RAM and 512GB of storage, featuring a 6.67-inch 120Hz AMOLED display with a holepunch 32-megapixel selfie camera and a night vision camera on the back, running Android 16.
