The Cursed Rise of Furby, the Toy That Captivated the World

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: The Verge

The Furby, a small, furry electronic toy, became a global phenomenon in the late 1990s, captivating the hearts of millions with its seemingly intelligent behavior and endearing appearance.

Despite its limited capabilities, the Furby's ability to look at its owner, speak nonsensical phrases, and appear to be aware of its surroundings made it a sensation, with people of all ages falling in love with the toy.

Advertisement

The story of Furby's creation is a testament to the power of innovation and the importance of rethinking human-computer interaction. From its humble beginnings in an off-grid house in California to its rise as the hottest toy of 1998, Furby represents a unique approach to AI and technology that continues to inspire and influence the industry today.

On this episode of Version History, a team of experts, including David Pierce, Victoria Song, and Sean Hollister, delve into the history of Furby, exploring its roots, its development, and the unusual programming and personality that made it a success.

As we reflect on the Furby phenomenon, we can't help but wonder what it means for the future of AI and technology. Will we see a resurgence of similar toys and devices that capture our hearts and imaginations?
