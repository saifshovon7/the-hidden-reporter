Smart Glasses Make Mainstream Debut: Tech Life Explores the Future of Wearables

The Hidden Reporter Staff February 10, 2026 Source: BBC Technology

Is this the year when smart glasses become mainstream? Tech firms are hoping so, with the latest devices hitting the market. Tech Life's Alasdair Keane has road tested some of these wearables to see if they're worth the investment.

From smart glasses to smart bricks, it's been a childhood favourite for decades, and now Lego is packing its colourful plastic with lights, sensors and sound synthesizers. The new tech has been put to the test by Tech Life's Shiona McCallum.

A Hong Kong tech company is also making waves with its development of AI for neglected languages. Starting with Cantonese, the technology aims to bridge the gap between communities and promote cultural understanding.
