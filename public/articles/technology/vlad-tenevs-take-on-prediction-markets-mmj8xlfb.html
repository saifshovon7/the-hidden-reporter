Vlad Tenev's Take on Prediction Markets

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: Bloomberg Technology

Vlad Tenev, CEO of Robinhood, recently joined the Odd Lots podcast to discuss his company's involvement in prediction markets. He shared his thoughts on the growth potential of betting markets and revealed his favorite bet, which may not be what investors expect.

The conversation covered various topics, including the impact of the conflict with Iran on oil prices and the US's decision to give India a pass on Russian oil. Tenev also touched on the Iranian regime's ability to survive a war and the potential for the conflict to serve as a market guide.

Additionally, the discussion touched on the stability of finance hubs in the Gulf region, Lloyd Blankfein's views on Elon Musk's backing of the US, and the potential impact of the Iran war on Dubai's status. Other topics included China's softening GDP growth target for 2026, the US's decision to sink an Iranian ship, and the implications of the conflict for India and Saudi Arabia.

Throughout the conversation, Tenev emphasized the importance of understanding the complexities of the situation and the potential risks involved in betting on war. He also highlighted the need for a nuanced approach to addressing the conflict and its potential consequences.
