Nintendo Switch Games on Sale for Mario's 40th Anniversary Celebrations

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: IGN

Nintendo is kicking off the festivities for fans in honor of the Super Mario Bros. 40th anniversary, alongside the upcoming release of the Super Mario Galaxy Movie.

Several Nintendo Switch games are discounted as part of the sale, alongside other exciting discounts. The sale includes Super Mario 3D World + Bowser's Fury and Super Mario Odyssey down to $39.99 each, alongside the Switch 2 Edition of Super Mario Party Jamboree + Jamboree TV for just $59.99.

Advertisement

The sale is available from March 8 to 14, and retailers in the US are offering discounts on Nintendo Switch games. Additionally, Nintendo is touting Super Mario Party Jamboree + Jamboree TV as the first Switch 2 game to be officially discounted.

Nintendo is also offering deals on select titles for digital-only gamers, such as Mario vs. Donkey Kong and Super Mario Maker 2 for $39.99 each. The sale is available through various retailers, including Amazon and Best Buy.

The Nintendo Switch 2 256GB memory card is also back down to its best price since Black Friday last year, at $39, a 35% discount off the $59.99 list price.
