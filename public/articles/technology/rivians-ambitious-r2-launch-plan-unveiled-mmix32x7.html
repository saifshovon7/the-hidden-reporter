Rivian's Ambitious R2 Launch Plan Unveiled

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: TechCrunch

Welcome back to TechCrunch Mobility, your hub for all things “future of transportation.”

Rivian is planning one of the fastest launches of a new all-electric car in the U.S. with its forthcoming R2 SUV.

The company expects to sell between 20,000 and 25,000 R2s this year, with the first units heading to customers in June once production begins.

This means Rivian has six months to reach its goal, a feat that will require a strategic approach to marketing and sales.

Advertisement

Senior reporter Sean O’Kane analyzed historical sales data and Rivian’s own projections for 2026, revealing that even if Rivian hits the low end of its target, its sales rate will outpace every other comparable electric vehicle at or under $60,000 aside from the Tesla Model Y.

Rivian's CFO Claire McDonough, along with other industry leaders, will be attending SXSW in Austin next week, where the company is expected to reveal full details of its R2 line.

The stakes are high, and Rivian will need to employ a variety of tactics to spread the word and turn interest in its EVs into actual sales.

As the company prepares for the launch, questions remain about what tactical move Rivian will use to reach its goal.

Experts and readers are invited to share their predictions and insights on Rivian's approach.
