Samsung Pursues AI Partnerships to Close Gap with Apple

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: Financial Times

Samsung is actively seeking deals that incorporate artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance its smartphones, as the company aims to challenge Apple's dominant market position.

The move comes as the global smartphone market continues to evolve, with AI-powered features becoming increasingly important to users.

Advertisement

Samsung's strategy involves partnering with various companies to develop and integrate AI capabilities into its devices, which could potentially give the company a competitive edge over Apple.

While the details of these partnerships remain private, industry experts believe that Samsung's efforts could lead to significant improvements in its smartphone offerings.

As the smartphone market continues to shift towards AI-driven features, Samsung's focus on partnerships and innovation may ultimately help the company close the gap with Apple.
