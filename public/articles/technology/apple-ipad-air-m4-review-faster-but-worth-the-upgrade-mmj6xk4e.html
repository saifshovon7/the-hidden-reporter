Apple iPad Air M4 Review: Faster, But Worth the Upgrade?

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: The Verge

The newest iPad Air is a chip bump iPad, boasting an M4 processor, a C1X cellular modem, and an N1 chip that brings Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, and Thread support.

Apple describes the Air as a product of its operations department, where top-line tech is retired from the iPad Pro and produced with more scale and cost-efficiency.

Advertisement

Despite some compromises, such as 128GB base storage and a 60Hz LED display, the iPad Air remains a compelling option for most people.

With a price of $599, it offers good tech at a good price, making it the recommended choice for those in the market for a new iPad.

However, some users may find the base iPad to be underpowered, and the Pro to be too expensive for their needs.
