Pokopia: A Refreshing Pokémon Spin-Off Surprising Reviewers

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 3, 2026 Source: BBC Technology

The Pokémon series has been a beloved franchise for decades, with a recent surge in popularity. However, some fans have grown dissatisfied with the series' recent offerings, citing issues with graphical performance, gameplay, and a perceived lack of innovation.

Enter Pokopia, a spin-off game that has landed on Nintendo's Switch 2 to almost universal acclaim. This game marks a departure from the series' traditional creature-collecting and battling style, instead embracing a life simulator format reminiscent of Animal Crossing and Stardew Valley.

Advertisement

In Pokopia, players take on the role of Ditto, a shape-shifting Pokémon who can mimic others, and must rebuild a post-apocalyptic Kanto region. The game challenges players to create suitable homes and habitats for other Pokémon, attend to their needs and wants, and unravel a mystery surrounding the disappearance of trainers.

Reviewers have praised Pokopia's unique premise, citing its excellent life simulation gameplay and complex mechanics. Jordan Middler from Video Games Chronicle awarded the game five stars, describing it as "an excellent life simulation game that takes the best bits from the champions of the genre."

IGN's Rebekah Valentine also gave a thumbs-up to the main character, Ditto, calling it "a little weirdo" that she loves. While not all reviewers were equally impressed, with Stacy Henley from TheGamer giving it three stars, Pokopia currently boasts an overall rating of 88 out of 100 on Metacritic, making it the best-reviewed title of 2026 so far.
