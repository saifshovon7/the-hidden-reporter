Apple's Unconventional TikTok Videos Resonate with Gen Z

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: MacRumors

Apple is promoting its MacBook Neo with a series of intentionally strange TikTok videos that are capturing the attention of Gen Z.

The videos, which have been posted in sets of three, correspond to the laptop's Blush, Citrus, and Indigo color options, as well as a fourth option, Silver.

The videos feature a mix of abstract and humorous content, including a lemon receiving a FaceTime call from a lime and iconic footage from the original Macintosh's introduction in 1984.

Advertisement

Some viewers have expressed confusion, while others have praised Apple's absurdist marketing strategy and the fact that the company is allowing comments on the videos.

Apple also showcased a mini 3D figure modeled after the Finder app icon during a live stream on TikTok, which has been dubbed "Little Finder Guy" on social media.

The MacBook Neo is priced as low as $499 in the U.S., making it an attractive option for young people.

The company's marketing strategy reflects its target audience and is likely to appeal to those who value creativity and individuality.
