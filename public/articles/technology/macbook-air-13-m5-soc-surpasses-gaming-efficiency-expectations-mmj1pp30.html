MacBook Air 13 M5 SoC Surpasses Gaming Efficiency Expectations

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: Notebookcheck.net

A recent review of the base model Apple MacBook Air 13, equipped with the M5 SoC, has revealed impressive gaming efficiency. The device's base configuration, featuring an M5 SoC with 8 GPU cores, demonstrates exceptional performance while consuming minimal power.

During gaming tests, the M5 SoC consumed between 7-8 Watts, with Cyberpunk 2077 and Baldur's Gate 3 showcasing consistent performance, reaching 19.6 fps in 1080p with ultra graphics settings. Notably, the SoC maintained this level of performance even after one hour of gameplay.

Advertisement

The MacBook Air's passive cooling system enables efficient operation, with the M5 SoC quickly stabilizing at 7-8 Watts under combined workloads. By reducing graphics details and utilizing MetalFX upscaling, users can enjoy smooth gameplay in demanding titles without experiencing throttling.

In comparison, the actively cooled MacBook Pro 14, featuring a 10-core GPU M5 SoC, consumes more power but offers enhanced gaming performance. The MacBook Air 13, with its efficient M5 SoC, stands out as the most efficient laptop in our database, delivering unplayable frame rates.

Notably, the entire system, including the display, measures just 19 Watts, further emphasizing the MacBook Air 13's exceptional efficiency. For more information, please refer to our in-depth review of the Apple MacBook Air 13 M5.
