Samsung's Mario-themed microSD card for Switch 2 discounted to 35% off

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 9, 2026 Source: The Verge

Nintendo has cut the cost of its Mario-themed Samsung microSD Express card with 256GB of storage, just in time for Mario Day.

Normally priced at $59.99, the card is now available for $39.99 at various retailers, including Amazon, Best Buy, GameStop, and Walmart.

The microSD Express card is a must-have for Switch 2 owners who are running out of storage space, as some games can consume up to 50GB of storage.

Advertisement

It's worth noting that microSD Express cards are only compatible with the Switch 2, and standard microSD cards will not work in the newer console for storing and playing games.

Currently, all microSD Express cards offer the same read and write speeds, so the main differences lie in capacity and price.

As a result, the most affordable option from a trusted company and retailer is the best choice for consumers.
