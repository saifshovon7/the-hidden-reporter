Google CEO Sundar Pichai Receives $692 Million Pay Package

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 8, 2026 Source: TechCrunch

Sundar Pichai's new pay package could be worth $692 million, according to a filing by Alphabet. The three-year deal for the Google CEO includes new stock incentives linked to Waymo and its drone delivery venture Wing.

The majority of Pichai's pay package is tied to performance, making him one of the highest-paid executives on the planet. This contrasts with the public fascination surrounding Google's founders, Larry Page and Sergey Brin, who have recently purchased lavish Miami properties.

Advertisement

Page reportedly spent over $173 million on two mansions in Coconut Grove, Florida, while Brin was linked to a $51 million megamansion. In contrast, Pichai remains quietly rooted in Los Altos, California, with a net worth estimated to be nearly $1.15 billion.

Google's market cap has grown significantly since Pichai took the helm in 2015, making the stock he's accumulated along the way hugely valuable. He and his wife currently hold shares worth nearly $500 million, with another estimated $650 million sold as of last summer, according to Bloomberg's calculations.
