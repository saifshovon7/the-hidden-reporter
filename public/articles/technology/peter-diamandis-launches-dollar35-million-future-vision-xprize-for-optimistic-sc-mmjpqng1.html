Peter Diamandis Launches $3.5 Million Future Vision Xprize for Optimistic Sci-Fi

Peter Diamandis has launched a new $3.5 million Future Vision Xprize to encourage optimistic sci-fi worlds. The contest aims to promote a positive vision of the future through film.

The Hidden Reporter Staff · March 9, 2026 · Source: TechCrunch

Famed Xprize founder Peter Diamandis has launched a new $3.5 million Future Vision Xprize to encourage more optimistic sci-fi worlds to come to our screens.

Diamandis credits his whole fabulous career to watching 'Star Trek' as a child growing up, citing the show's hopeful vision of the future as the motivation behind his achievements.

He finds that sci-fi movies and TV shows these days are largely fixated on calamity, painting a dystopian vision of the future.

Advertisement

'Why would you ever want to live in that future?' Diamandis said. 'I truly believe that if we see a positive vision of the future, we will build it.'

The contest aims to encourage film creators to tell more stories about how good things can be in a technological future.

Diamandis believes that the speed of change makes it hard for people to envision their futures, especially when clobbered with 'negative stories about tomorrow.'

However, he notes that it's never been easier for anyone with an idea to pursue it, with the most powerful tools on the planet being free and available to everybody.
