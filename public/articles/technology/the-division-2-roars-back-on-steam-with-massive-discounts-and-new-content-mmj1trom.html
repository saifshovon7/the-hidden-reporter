The Division 2 Roars Back on Steam with Massive Discounts and New Content

The Hidden Reporter Staff March 7, 2026 Source: Kotaku

The Division 2 is experiencing a resurgence on Steam this weekend, thanks to a significant discount and a recent roadmap outlining the game's future content.

An anniversary event has added fresh rewards, a new realism mode that emphasizes headshots over gear score, and a month-long battle pass with new rewards to grind for.

Advertisement

The realism mode, a timed event, is only available in the Warlords of New York campaign and features a reduced interface, recovered ammo from defeated enemies, and skills with longer cooldowns and added risk.

Ubisoft has also announced a "Rise Up" update for April, which will increase player power and overall difficulty, as well as bring PvP balancing, crossplay, and new content.

The hype surrounding the game's anniversary has propelled The Division 2 back up the Steam charts, with concurrent numbers increasing 400 percent higher than normal.

The game is currently available on Steam for $3, a 90 percent discount, and the first game can be purchased for $1.40 more.
